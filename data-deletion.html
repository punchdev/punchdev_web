Data Deletion Request

Last updated: May 17, 2026

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data held by PunchDev Agency. We take data privacy seriously and will process your request within 30 days of verification.

What Data We Collect

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Company name and business details
  • Website usage data (cookies, Meta Pixel)
  • Communication records (calls, SMS, emails)
  • Lead form submissions from our ads

What Happens When You Request Deletion

  • We verify your identity using the email or phone on file
  • All personal data is permanently removed from our CRM (GoHighLevel)
  • Your data is removed from Meta Custom Audiences
  • Any lead records associated with your information are deleted
  • You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

Request Data Deletion

Request Submitted

We've received your data deletion request. We'll verify your identity and complete the deletion within 30 days. You'll receive a confirmation email at the address provided.

Note: Some data may be retained as required by law (e.g., financial records for tax purposes). Data shared with our clients (e.g., lead information) will be flagged for deletion with the respective client. If you interacted with us through a Facebook ad, you can also manage your data through Facebook's Business Tools settings.

Alternative Request Methods

You can also request data deletion by emailing us directly at privacy@punchdev.net with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and including your name and email address.
