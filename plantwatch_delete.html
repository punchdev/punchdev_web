Delete Your Account

Request deletion of your PlantWatch account and all associated data.

How it works

  1. Fill out the form below using the email address linked to your PlantWatch account.
  2. Choose whether you want your full account deleted or only specific data types.
  3. Submit — we'll confirm by email and process your request within 30 days.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, we remove:

  • Your account credentials and login email
  • All plant entries, photos, notes, reminders, and care logs
  • In-app preferences and settings

The following may be retained temporarily where required by law:

  • Billing and transaction records (if applicable)
  • Security and fraud-prevention logs
We aim to delete or irreversibly anonymize all personal data within 30 days of confirming your request.
