PlantWatch account & data deletion

This page is for users of the PlantWatch app who want to request deletion of their app account and associated data. [web:16][web:19][web:22]

How to request deletion

  1. Fill out the form below using the same email address you use in the PlantWatch app.
  2. Tell us whether you want your account and all associated data deleted or only specific data types. [web:16][web:19][web:25]
  3. Submit the form. You will receive a confirmation email or in‑app notification when your request has been processed.

What data is deleted or kept

When you request deletion of your PlantWatch account, the following data will normally be deleted:

  • Account identifiers (such as your PlantWatch account ID and login email stored in the app backend).
  • Profile data (display name, avatar or profile photo if stored by PlantWatch).
  • Plant tracking content (plant entries, notes, reminders, schedules, logs, and other activity saved in your account).
  • In‑app preferences (app settings associated with your account).

The following data may be retained for a limited period where required for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance (for example, tax or accounting rules): [web:17][web:20][web:22]

  • Basic transaction and billing records (if you made purchases or subscriptions through PlantWatch).
  • Security, audit, and abuse‑prevention logs.

Unless a longer retention period is legally required, we aim to delete or irreversibly anonymize personal data within 30 days of confirming your request. [web:16][web:17][web:22]
